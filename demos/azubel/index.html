About Dr Rodrigo Azubel

Trained in Buenos Aires (1999) with experience in Argentina and China, Dr Rodrigo has been accredited by the Australian Dental Council since 2007. He believes in ongoing education and has taught in Oral Surgery, Community Dentistry, and care for high-risk patients. His advanced training includes Periodontics, Oral Rehabilitation, and a postgraduate program in Implantology (ITI).

  • Member: Australian Dental Association, Melbourne Seattle Study Club
  • Member: Australian Osseointegration Society, International Team for Implantology (ITI)
  • Patient-centred approach with comprehensive diagnosis and personalised plans

Outside the clinic, he enjoys time with his family and staying active through running, cycling, and kayaking.
